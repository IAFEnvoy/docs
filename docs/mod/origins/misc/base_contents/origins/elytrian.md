# Elytrian

**Impact:** <span style={{color:'green'}}>1 (Low)</span> | **Order:** 3 | **Icon:** Elytra

Often flying around in the winds, Elytrians are uncomfortable when they don't have enough space above their head.

---

## Powers

| Name | Power ID | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Winged | `origins:elytra` | You have Elytra wings without needing to equip any. |
| Gift of the Winds | `origins:launch_into_air` | Every 30 seconds, you are able to launch about 20 blocks up into the air. |
| Aerial Combatant | `origins:aerial_combatant` | You deal substantially more damage while in Elytra flight. |
| Need for Mobility | `origins:light_armor` | You can not wear any heavy armor (armor with protection values higher than chainmail). |
| Claustrophobia | `origins:claustrophobia` | Being somewhere with a low ceiling for too long will weaken you and make you slower. |
| Brittle Bones | `origins:more_kinetic_damage` | You take more damage from falling and flying into blocks. |
