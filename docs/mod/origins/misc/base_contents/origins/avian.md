# Avian

**Impact:** <span style={{color:'green'}}>1 (Low)</span> | **Order:** 0 | **Icon:** Feather

The Avian race has lost their ability to fly a long time ago. Now these peaceful creatures can be seen gliding from one place to another.

---

## Powers

| Name | Power ID | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Featherweight | `origins:slow_falling` | You fall as gently as a feather, taking no fall damage. |
| Fresh Air | `origins:fresh_air` | When sleeping, your bed must be at an altitude of at least 86 blocks to allow respiration. |
| Like Air | `origins:like_air` | Modifiers to your walking speed also apply while you're airborne. |
| Tailwind | `origins:tailwind` | You are a little bit quicker on foot than others. |
| Oviparous | `origins:lay_eggs` | Whenever you wake up in the morning, you will lay an egg. |
