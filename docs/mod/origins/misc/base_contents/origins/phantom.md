# Phantom

**Impact:** <span style={{color:'red'}}>3 (High)</span> | **Order:** 2 | **Icon:** Phantom Membrane

As half-human and half-phantom beings, these creatures can switch between a Phantom and a normal form.

---

## Powers

| Name | Power ID | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Phantomize | `origins:phantomize/toggle` | You can switch between human and phantom form at will using your primary active key. |
| Phantomize Overlay | `origins:phantomize/overlay/green` | While phantomized, a green overlay appears. |
| Translucent | `origins:translucent` | While phantomized, your body becomes translucent. |
| Phasing | `origins:phasing` | While phantomized, you can walk through solid blocks (except those in the `origins:unphasable` block tag). |
| Invisibility | `origins:invisibility` | While phantomized, you are invisible to other players and mobs. |
| Photoallergic | `origins:burn_in_daylight` | You begin to burn in daylight if you are not invisible. |
| Fast Metabolism | `origins:hunger_over_time` | Being phantomized causes you to exhaust over time. |
| Fragile | `origins:fragile` | You have 3 fewer hearts of health than humans. |
