# Blazeborn

**Impact:** <span style={{color:'red'}}>3 (High)</span> | **Order:** 1 | **Icon:** Blaze Powder

Late descendants of the Blaze, the Blazeborn are naturally immune to the perils of the Nether.

---

## Powers

| Name | Power ID | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Fire Immunity | `origins:fire_immunity` | You are immune to all types of fire damage. |
| Nether Inhabitant | `origins:nether_spawn` | Your natural spawn will be in the Nether. |
| Burning Wrath | `origins:burning_wrath` | When on fire, you deal additional damage with your attacks. |
| Hotblooded | `origins:hotblooded` | Due to your hot body, venoms burn up, making you immune to poison and hunger status effects. |
| Hydrophobia | `origins:water_vulnerability` | You receive damage over time while in contact with water. |
| Flame Particles | `origins:flame_particles` | Flame particles surround you. |
