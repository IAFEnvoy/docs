# Arachnid

**Impact:** <span style={{color:'green'}}>1 (Low)</span> | **Order:** 1 | **Icon:** Cobweb

Their climbing abilities and the ability to trap their foes in spiderweb make the Arachnid perfect hunters.

---

## Powers

| Name | Power ID | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Climbing | `origins:climbing` | You are able to climb up any kind of wall, not just ladders. |
| Master of Webs | `origins:master_of_webs/climbing` | You can climb up any wall with ease. |
| Master of Webs | `origins:master_of_webs/no_slowdown` | You do not get slowed down by cobwebs. |
| Master of Webs | `origins:master_of_webs/punch_through` | You can punch through cobwebs without breaking them. |
| Master of Webs | `origins:master_of_webs/sense` | You sense which entities are stuck in cobwebs around you. |
| Master of Webs | `origins:master_of_webs/web_crafting` | You can craft cobwebs from string. |
| Master of Webs | `origins:master_of_webs/webbing` | You can trap enemies in a cobweb with your primary active power key. |
| Carnivore | `origins:carnivore` | Your diet is restricted to meat, you can't eat vegetables. |
| Fragile | `origins:fragile` | You have 3 fewer hearts of health than humans. |
