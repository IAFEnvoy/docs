# Enderian

**Impact:** <span style={{color:'orange'}}>2 (Medium)</span> | **Order:** 1 | **Icon:** Ender Pearl

Born as children of the Ender Dragon, Enderians are capable of teleporting but are vulnerable to water.

---

## Powers

| Name | Power ID | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Teleportation | `origins:throw_ender_pearl` | Whenever you want, you may throw an ender pearl which deals no damage, allowing you to teleport. |
| Hydrophobia | `origins:water_vulnerability` | You receive damage over time while in contact with water. |
| Scared of Gourds | `origins:pumpkin_hate/prevent_eating` | You are not able to eat pumpkin-related foods. |
| Scared of Gourds | `origins:pumpkin_hate/prevent_seeing` | You can not see a player while they are wearing a carved pumpkin on their head. |
| Slender Body | `origins:extra_reach` | You can reach blocks and entities further away. |
| Ender Particles | `origins:ender_particles` | Ender particles surround you. |
