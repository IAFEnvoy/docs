# Shulk

**Impact:** <span style={{color:'green'}}>1 (Low)</span> | **Order:** 4 | **Icon:** Shulker Shell

Related to Shulkers, the bodies of the Shulk are outfitted with a protective shell-like skin.

---

## Powers

| Name | Power ID | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Hoarder | `origins:shulker_inventory` | You have access to an additional 9 inventory slots, which are kept on death. |
| Sturdy Skin | `origins:natural_armor` | Even without wearing armor, your skin provides natural protection. |
| Strong Arms | `origins:strong_arms/break_speed` | You are able to break natural stones without a pickaxe. |
| Strong Arms | `origins:strong_arms/break_stone` | Breaking speed for natural stones is increased. |
| No Shield | `origins:no_shield` | You cannot hold a shield with your offhand. |
| Large Appetite | `origins:more_exhaustion` | You exhaust much quicker than others, thus requiring you to eat more. |
